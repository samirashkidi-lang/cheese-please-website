Our Story

Tampa's Hidden Gem for Cheese & Wine Lovers

Tucked away on S MacDill Ave in South Tampa, we're the only dedicated gourmet cheese store in all of Tampa Bay — and we're proud of it. We carry the largest selection of international cheeses in the region, imported directly from France, Spain, Holland, Italy, and beyond.

We're not just a cheese shop. We're a full wine bar, cocktail bar, and charcuterie destination. Whether you're planning a date night, a girls' night out, or just want to treat yourself to something extraordinary — this is your spot.
